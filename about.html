ABOUT US

PV Designs believes in creating an aesthetic and unique design in the most creative yet simple manner. The firm believes in teamwork being their strongest foundation.

Our goal is to provide architectural and design services to the best of our ability giving our priority to interpersonal client relations and client satisfaction.

As a firm, we are committed to and strive for:

  • Creating unique and innovative designs based on client requirements.
  • Prompt and timely delivery of our services.
  • A professional ethic with values such as honesty, integrity, accountability and respect among and for our clients, colleagues, commitments and environment.

Chetan Vora (Interior Designer)

Co-founder

Chetan Vora began his career in Mumbai in 1990 under the banner Chetan Enterprise established to carry out various interior design works and to cater to specialized repairs in waterproofing and renovation of buildings and structures.

Today, Chetan Enterprise and PV Designs have collaborated together to provide more creative solutions and much better services not only as founder and co-founder but also as a father-son duo to take their company to new heights and complete client satisfaction.

After a period of continued dedicated efforts the company is distinguished for utilization of well adapted technologies in areas of interior design, architectural design, repair and waterproofing which result in longer life with high functional and aesthetic values.

A number of prestigious projects have been carried out under the supervision of their team personnel for ensuring guaranteed long lasting service with assurance of their quality services and careful attention at all times.
